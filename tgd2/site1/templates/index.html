About Us

Side-View Hotel & Spa is a family-friendly hotel that offers a wide range of accommodation types, from rooms to suites. All this in the peaceful surroundings of our beautiful gardens, will make your time at Khwisero an unforgettable holiday.
Esteemed guests are welcome to relax and unwind in a quiet and elegant setting in the popular resort of Khwisero and enjoy a genuine experience of leisure, pleasure, gastronomy and wellness within the premises of the resort
